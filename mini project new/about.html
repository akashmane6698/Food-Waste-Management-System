About Us

An Initiative by M. Sc. (IT), DAIICT Students

Waste food management and donation website have been developed to provide food to the people in need from the people or organizations where there is already some food available that is going to waste. Therefore, simultaneously this web application is also beneficial in reducing the food waste problem.
